About Us

Chef Angy

Passionate about cooking since childhood, Angy whose real name is LOUHOU Angélina, reinvents African classics daily with a modern and creative touch. Her fusion approach blends the richness of traditional flavors with the elegance of contemporary presentation. Every dish from her kitchen is a love letter to Afro culinary culture.

Our Founder

Behind Angy Spice lies a vision: to showcase African cuisine with excellence, discipline, and heart. As CEO, Angy wears two hats—chef and strategist. She masterfully oversees quality, brand identity, and the authentic connection with our clients.

Catering

At Angy Spice, our catering service brings authentic Congolese and African flavors to your events. Whether for business meetings, birthday parties, or private dinners, we offer generous, homemade meal boxes crafted with care. Each box features a balanced mix of rice or attiéké, sautéed vegetables, and perfectly seasoned meats or fish. Our meals are prepared fresh, delivered promptly, and always beautifully presented—because every bite should feel like home.
